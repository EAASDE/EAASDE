About Us

Dr. Lyvia Leigh is a board-certified allergist and clinical immunologist.

Training:

  • Medical School: New York University (NYU) School of Medicine
  • Residency in Internal Medicine: New York University (NYU) School of Medicine
  • Fellowship in Allergy & Immunology: Hospital of the University of Pennsylvania (UPenn) and Children's Hospital of Philadelphia (CHOP)


She has published and co-authored papers and textbook chapters.  After training, she practiced in the Metropolitan Philadelphia area for several years before moving to Sussex County, Delaware in 2021. She practiced in Georgetown, DE before deciding to start her own allergy practice in Lewes, DE.


Her areas of expertise include:

  • Allergic rhinitis and conjunctivitis (hayfever)
  • Asthma
  • Atopic dermatitis (eczema)
  • Anaphylaxis
  • Food allergy
  • Drug allergy
  • Contact allergy
  • Stinging insect allergy
  • Hives
  • Swelling (angioedema)
  • Chronic/recurrent sinusitis
  • Nasal polyps
  • Chronic cough
  • Eosinophilic esophagitis
  • Primary immunodeficiency


She sees pediatric and adult patients of all ages. She is fluent in English, Spanish, and Mandarin.
